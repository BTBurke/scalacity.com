About Scalacity
We're committed to helping you build high performance data-driven applications. We're a team with lots of experience working on hard problems for the military and the intelligence community, two organizations that deal with enormous amounts of data and have stringent performance requirements. Setting up an architecture that scales and gives you the performance you want is tedious. Let us help you do that so you can concentrate on getting value out of your data.

We're a startup with roots in the DC entrepreneurship community. You can help us help you by getting in touch and telling us about your business, your application, and the problems that you have. We'd love to hear from you.
